Integrate our online car rental software into your rent-a-car website. This powerful car rental script will streamline the management of bookings, payments, and vehicles. Below is a list of features supported by default. We can add any other functionality to the Car Rental System upon request.


Car rental script is a powerful web based software for those, who provide car rental service. We can also make any custom modification that you may need. By offering highly-customizable Car rental script , your customers will be able to see vehicle availability, and make online reservations from your website, with just a few clicks. KEY FEATURES


Simple Car Management

The user-friendly car booking system allows you to manage your car fleet, add car types, set extras availability, and more.

Car Booking Management

The built-in rental software allows admins to add, edit, and delete bookings, manage customer data and locations.

Rent-a-car Automation

Our high-performance Car Rental System allows you to set your own booking rules and automate the reservation process.



Online & Offline Payments

Collect various types of payments available in the car rental software - PayPal, Authorize.Net, CC, wire transfers, cash, etc.

Extra Services

Assign different extras to each car (child seat, GPS, full insurance, etc.) The price is calculated per day or per reservation.

Email & SMS Notification

Personalize and send out various types of Email and SMS auto-responder messages to both customers and administrators.
